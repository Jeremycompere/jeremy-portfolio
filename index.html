The Compere

About Jeremy

02

Jeremiah Ibiwoye, known as Jeremy, is a compere and event host whose stage presence spans orchestra concert halls, children's festival grounds, TEDx stages, and wedding venues across Nigeria. He brings a rare combination of energy management, crowd reading, and event awareness that lets every programme breathe naturally — no matter the scale.

His approach is rooted in preparation and authenticity — studying every event brief, understanding the audience, and delivering a performance that feels both spontaneous and precisely calibrated. From back-to-back editions of Toontopia at the National Theatre to the grandeur of ZAMAR — Ilorin's largest orchestra concert — to four consecutive days on the Wiflow Africa Music Masterclass stage, Jeremy has proven his range across genres, formats, and audiences.

Whether he's holding the room at a TEDx conference themed Rewriting Limits or guiding a couple's most important day with warmth and precision, the goal is always the same: make it feel effortless for the audience, while doing all the heavy lifting behind the mic.

Energy & Crowd Control
Reads the room and adapts tone in real time — from high-voltage festival MC to measured, warm wedding host.
Programme Management
Keeps complex multi-act events on schedule without the audience ever noticing the logistics.
Audience Versatility
